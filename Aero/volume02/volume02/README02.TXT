* FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)                                    *
*********************************************************************** 

Is the data available through FTP or on the World Wide Web?

The data is also available on the Internet from the host
opus.aae.uiuc.edu using anonymous FTP.  See the file
/pub/lsat/AIRFOIL.DATA for directions on which files to copy.  Also
the data can be found on the World Wide Web by starting at the site
http://www.uiuc.edu/ph/www/m-selig/ and follow the links to the UIUC
Low Speed Airfoil Tests.
 
Is the data available on diskettes?

Yes, all of the airfoil coordinates and performance data presented are
available on IBM and Macintosh compatible diskettes through SoarTech
Publications.  SoarTech will be returning a portion of the proceeds
from all disk and book sales to help support the continuation of these
airfoil wind tunnel tests.  These funds are vital and provide a
significant portion of the total project funding.  Write to:

SoarTech Publications
c/o Herk Stokely
1504 N. Horseshoe Circle
Virginia Beach, VA 23451
email: herkstok@aol.com

Is pitching moment data available?

Not for the Volume 1 and 2 data sets, but it is available for Vol 3.

The airfoil performance data is copyrighted, and restrictions are
placed on its use.  The data may be freely copied and used in any way,
e.g., for personal use, in magazine articles or with a commercial
product.  If the data is used in a magazine article or book, the
author must reference the appropriate volume of Summary of Low-Speed
Airfoil Data and give the address of where it can be obtained.  If the
data is used in a commercial product, there must be no extra charges
for providing it - other than the cost of reproduction and
distribution.  All products that make use of this data must
conspicuously state that it was produced under the UIUC Low-Speed
Airfoil Test program, and no restrictions can be placed on the
recipient with respect to their use of the source data.  Furthermore,
they must be allowed to copy the original data and freely distribute
it as well.  It is in this sense that all access to this data is free.
More details can be found in the general public license (file:
GPL.TXT).  A copy of the license, the copyright notice and the UIUC
Low-Speed Airfoil Tests Manifesto (file: MANIFEST.TXT which ever is
most recent) must be included with each distribution of the data.
